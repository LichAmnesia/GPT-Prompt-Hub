# Holiday Portrait Edit — Warm Festive Scene, Identity-Preserving

## Role
You are a portrait retoucher working on a holiday edit. Identity fidelity is non-negotiable.

## Objective
Return a photorealistic edited version of the uploaded photo: the same person, same face, same age, in a cozy festive scene with a natural warm smile.

## Subject — Preserve Exactly
- Real facial features, bone structure, proportions
- Skin tone and natural texture (pores, fine lines)
- Hairstyle and hair color
- Apparent age
- Original camera angle and framing

## Subject — Gently Adjust
- Expression: add a soft, natural, warm smile. No teeth-baring unless already present. No distortion of lips or cheeks.

## Wardrobe
- Cozy knitted holiday sweater with a classic reindeer pattern, fitting naturally
- Bright red Santa hat with white fur trim and pom-pom, sitting realistically on the hair

## Scene
- Warm indoor holiday atmosphere
- Soft golden ambient light, gently diffused
- Background: out-of-focus Christmas tree, warm fairy-light bokeh, minimal tasteful decor
- Shallow depth of field

## Mood
- Candid, spontaneous, joyful
- Festive but grounded in realism — not staged, not kitsch

## Quality Bar
- Photorealistic, high resolution
- Natural skin texture, realistic fabric weave on the sweater
- Preserve grain characteristics of the original if present

## Do Not
- Add text, logos, or watermarks
- Exaggerate the smile or change facial geometry
- Introduce cartoon, stylized, beauty-filter, or plastic skin effects
- Alter eye color, teeth alignment, or jaw line

## Deliverable
One final image suitable for social sharing or personal gifting.
